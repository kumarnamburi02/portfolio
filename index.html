Stress Detection

The Stress Detection project aims to identify and analyze stress levels in individuals using various data inputs and algorithms. By gathering data through surveys and user inputs, I created a comprehensive dataset that served as the foundation for the analysis. I implemented machine learning algorithms to accurately classify stress levels based on factors such as emotional state, workload, and lifestyle choices. Additionally, I developed a user-friendly interface that allows users to input their data and receive feedback on their stress levels. The project also provides insights into stress patterns and offers recommendations for effective stress management. This experience not only enhanced my technical skills in programming and data analysis but also deepened my understanding of mental health and the importance of stress management.
